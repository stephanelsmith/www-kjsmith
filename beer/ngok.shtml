Ngok' Beer

Brewed in Pointe Noire, the Congo, this label is a common sight on tables at the Plage Sportif resturant where my colligues and I would decompress after long hours reviewing geological data in the Hydro-Congo office. This brew adds to the memory of a special visit I made east of Brazzaville to visit a local personality on the Mayombe plateau.

It is a fairly strong beer brewed in one of the two breweries of the Congo. The label I find just great with a strong clean design immediately recognizable.
