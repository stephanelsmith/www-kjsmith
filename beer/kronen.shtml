Kronenbourg

Kronenbourg, now a suburb of Strasbourg, lies a couple of klicks from the village of Illkirch-Graffenstaden where my mother-in-law is from. A crown jewel of Alsace, it is a strong lager with a well earned reputation as France's premier beer. This label was taken from an export bottle and really needs to be replaced by the real 1664 label.
