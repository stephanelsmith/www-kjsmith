From Alexey Kovalev in Belarus

Klinskoye Zolotoye Beer, or Klin Gold, from Klin in Russia just a bit across the border from Belarus and the home of Alexey in Gomel', or Homel' as some maps now show. My Russian friend and colligue Yulia Garipova assures me that this beer is well known with a good reputation.
Contributing to this page from Belarus is Alexey Kovalev with a pitcher of Belarussian label, and this fine Russian label. Alexey collects and trades labels from many places and may be reached by email at: axel99@mail.ru My sincere thanks to Alexey for the labels.
