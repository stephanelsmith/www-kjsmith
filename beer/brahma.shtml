Chopp Brahma, Brazil

Chopp Brahma beer is a great brew, (chopp = draft) which I enjoyed recently in Sao Paulo and Rio on a family vacation. The best of all was Chopp Brahma Black, only on drafy so no label, but an incredible stout! Sorry Guiness, but this was the creamiest, smoothest, best stout I have partaken. My hat's off to the Brazilian brewing skills.
