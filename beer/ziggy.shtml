From Bud Meyers, Long time beer drinker

My thanks to Bud for his fine contribution to my page. Bud passed a milestone recently in his beer drinking career which he shares with us in his own words:

I like your site! I'm a long-time beer drinker, and began collecting labels about five years ago. The label I'm attaching (well, a digital photo of it) was from my one thousandth different beer that I can prove. Many of the ones I had years ago are no longer brewed, some bottles had painted labels, and a few labels were simply impossible to remove.

The beer itself was mediocre; but it'll always be special to me because I'll never live long enough to have a two thousandth.

Bud
