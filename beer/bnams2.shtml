Beer labels from North America

From Dave Edgar in Seattle comes this collection presenting a fantastic set of labels from all over North America. These labels have never been attached to a bottle, a true collection from a true collector. They were donated to me via my brewer brother from Dave Edgar, a brewer friend who seriously collects labels. While the "never used" condition kinda goes against my philosophical requirement that a label is only as good as the brew in the bottle, I am no purist and these are great labels, worth to be seen. My sincere thanks to Dave for the collection I proudly display on the following pages....
