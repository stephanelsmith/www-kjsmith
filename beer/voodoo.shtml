Blackened Voodoo Beer

From way down yonder in New Orleans the Dixie Brewing Company produces this fine beer with its fine label, Blackened Voodoo. Bryan Lastraps contributes this label after a recent holiday down dixie-way. He didnt say that much about the beer, but the label is just SO cool.. The scan really dosnt do it justice as it has a metallic silver backing upon which the design is printed.
