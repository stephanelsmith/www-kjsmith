From Radostaw Kwiecien in Poland

Contributing to this page from Poland is Radostaw (Radek) Kwiecien with several Polish labels. He assures me that the Polish brews are very good and may possibly be found in America as well, so now I have an assignment to track down and try them for myself!
Radek is a serious beer label collector and is always interested in new and exotic locals. If interested, email him at: kwiecien@zeus.polsl.gliwice.pl or look up his page at: http://www.polsl.gliwice.pl/~kwiecien
My hearty thanks to you Radek for the labels.
