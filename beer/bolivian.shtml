Paceña Tropical Extra

Paceña Tropical Extra. This label we found less often that the more typical Centenario. I am not sure I noticed the difference in taste but then, I never attempted a comparison. An excellent beer which accompanied many evening conversations under tropical stars.

You can visit the official CBN (Cervecería Boliviana Nacional S.A.) website at www.cbn-bo.com My thanks to Richard Melgarejo, CBN webmaster for providing the link.
