Rainier Beer

This label, dated from the 1970's or earlier, was a common sight in bars and refriderators across Washington State. The brew is a standard lager, relatively light, pretty good stuff. Memories of many good times with family and friends are evoked by this label. Also, my brother John Francis was a brewmaster (in charge of the brewing) at Rainier for many years so there is a family connection as well.
