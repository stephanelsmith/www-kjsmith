Shiner Summer Stock

A contribution from Davis B in east Texas, Home of Red Hills, Green Pines, and Bluebonnets. This label results from an impromptu email "chat" with Davis one evening as I was surfing about the web. He emailed me asking how to remove the label. What followed were several replies as he worked off a label and then this great scan.
So, what about the beer? In his own words, "I was just finishing up a SHINER SUMMER STOCK (a Old German Kolsch Beer - whatever the heck that is - blends Wheat and Barley Malt) ... a little heavier than their HONEY brew .... little more widespread than a true micro ..... interesting though." Many thanks for the effort to get that label off the bottle!
