Nighthawk, Katy, TX

Photographing birds as they fly overhead, sometimes quite high, is a thankless task. Mostly I get blurry images I can barely identify the bird in. Sometimes better.

Reslts from two seasons of evening nighthawk watching. The migration periods are clearly seen in this chart. Note, I have chosen to indicated in small red dots those evenings where I was making observations but no nighthawks were seen. This needs to be distinguished from evening where I was not making observations.
