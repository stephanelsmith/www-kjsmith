White Ibis, Katy, TX

When Houston receives a lot of heavy rain the catchment reservoirs fill and the waders come out in force. Still, photographing them is non-trivial as auto-focus does not work with so much sky. I must put the lens in manual-focus mode and say a prayer. Here, as a flight of Ibis's passed directly overhead I turned the lens up while swiveling around and hoped for the best. Sometimes it works!
