Chocard, Savoie

A Chocard (Chocard a Bec Jaune) is truly a bird of the high Alps, they make their homes at elevations ranging from 1000m up to nearly 4000m high. This fellow was spotted lurking about the Saulire at 2700m, hoping to snag a handout from the many skiers who invade the heights in winter.
