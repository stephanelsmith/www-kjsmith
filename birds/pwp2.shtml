Pileated Woodpecker, Katy


I was very surprised one day in 2012 when a magnificent Pileated Woodpecker flew into the garden and landed near the base of our large oak. I did not have my camera out and was upset not to have an opportunity to photograph the bird. Two years on we finally received a return visit and this time with a little luck she did pose for a photo. Do not wait so long for your next visit my lady friend!
