Hooded Warbler, Katy, Tx

On 19 March this cute little cousin of Mr Wilson showed up at the garden pool for a dip. He (Mr Hoodie) has become a regular customer in the garden, mostly searching for tasty bugs amoung the shrubs at the distant end of the garden. Welcome Mr Hoodie, we love to have you with us.

Apologies for the fuzzy photos. Low light and big lenses coupled with manual focus and, well, I am doing my best!
