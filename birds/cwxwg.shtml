Cedar Waxwing, Katy, Tx

In late winter we host flocks of Cedar Waxings, sometimes 30 - 40 or more birds. They take over the trees in and behind the garden and sit importantly... as waxwings are wont to do.

Cedar Waxwings KNOW they are cool.....

Cedar Waxwings are winter visitors to the Gulf Coast, infrequently seen from my garden but when they show, they appear in flocks.
