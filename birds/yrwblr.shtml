Yellow-Rumped Warbler, Houston

A recent occasional visitor to the swimming pool, this little sweetheart discreetly turns her back, her yellow petticoat peeking out below her wings.

Down on Mason Creek just a couple hundred yards away as the warbler flies, this little birdie perhaps envies its more citified cousin at the feeder.

Another winter visitor to the garden, these birds are otherwise resident in the Katy area.
