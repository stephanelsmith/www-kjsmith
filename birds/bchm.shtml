Black Chinned Hummingbird, Katy, Tx

Winter hummingbirds here in west Houston are devilishly difficult to identify, so much variety of immature males, females, and different overwintering species hanging around. All very confusing. This photo shows an immature Black Chinned (confirmed ID) visiting the feeder this January. Perhaps he will stick around after his gorget is fully developed and grace our summer garden.
