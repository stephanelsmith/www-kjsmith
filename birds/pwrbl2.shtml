Pine Warblers, Variations on a Theme, Katy

Winter 2013-2014 and the warblers are really filling the garden. In addition to 4 or more Orange Crowneds' and no end of Yellow Rumped warblers, we have 6 or so Pine warblers visiting all day every day. There are two adult males, maybe two adult females and juvinal birds as well. Each look a bit different, all the same.. Kaufman recommends getting to know your bird plumages well and I believe there is nothing quite so adapted for this as a well-stocked garden feeder.

Stats indicate another winter-only visitor to Katy Tx.
