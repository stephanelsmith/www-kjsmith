Sittelle Torchepot, Alsace

A Sittelle Torchepot, or common Nuthatch forages for grains and other goodies in the shrubbery outside my In-Law's house in Colmar, France. This little creature showed up with a small mixed flock of Mesanges and Moineaus (Chickadees and English Sparrows). Looks like it was enjoying some success!
