Garden Feeder, Houston

A small house finch in white culottes waits patiently her turn at the feeder.

Things can get a little hectic at the feeder, especially in winter when we can get upwards of ten or more species of bird visiting for a bite to eat. Finches and Sparrows invade in flocks. Its all lots of fun and non-stop action much of the time. I highly recommend this!
