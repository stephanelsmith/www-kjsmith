China Fantasy

I have had the fortunate luck to make many visits to China over the last 7 years. My travels have taken me across the northern stretches of China from Urumqi in central asia to Shanghai in the east and Harbin in the northeast. Along the way I have come to know the wonderful hospitality of the Chinese people, their love of eating, and the deep history they draw upon to form their culture.

It is impossible to capture the flavor and visual variety of China in only a few photos, but I try to select a few that are special to me in order to give an impression, through my eyes, of my many adventures in the middle kingdom.
