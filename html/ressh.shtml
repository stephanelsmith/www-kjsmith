Shell USA work experience summary.

January 1981 to July 1981
Powder River Basin Project: Focus on the Frontier Formation as a producing horizon and exploration target. Field study of the Spearhead Ranch / Powell complex.

July 1981 to December 1984
Wyoming Folded Belt Project: Study on the regional stratigraphy of the Mesozoic section and its influence on the thrust-fold styles in the provence. Study on the detailed structure of the northern portion of the Wyoming Folded Belt. Study on the Central and Southern structure of the Wyoming Folded Belt. "Patch Analog" study of the producing fairway from Pineview field to Whitney Canyon field. Geochemical and thermal maturation study of the Phosphoria formation.

January 1985 to September 1985
Central Utah / Utah Folded Belt Project: Integrated basin analysis from the Wasatch plateau to the Great Basin across the Folded Belt utilizing seismic mapping, construction of balanced and restored structural cross-sections, geochemical analysis and thermal maturation modeling, Play / Prospect definition.

October 1985 to June 1987
Columbia River Basin Project: Basin analysis study based on the data gathered in the first phase of drilling in the basin. Integration of all data and acquisition of new seismic data in preparation of second, and final, phase of drilling operations.

July 1987 to November 1989
Paradox Basin Project: Structural and stratigraphic evolution of the basin, its Pennsylvanian carbonate mounds, and salt dome piercement structures. Oil to source-rock typing study of the numerous productive zones found in the basin.

December 1989 to June 1991
Sub-Andean Basins Study, Ecuador, Peru, and Bolivia: Evolution of the Oriente basins with emphasis on the timing of oil maturation, expulsion, and migration with respect to the development of structural traps.

June 1991 to October 1995
North Africa Project: Complete hydrocarbon province characterization study contract for the Algerian National Oil Company including the stratigraphy and sedimentology of the Sahara Platform. Egypt Nile Delta bid round study made in partnership with Shell Egypt.

November 1995 to Present
West Africa Project: Congo Basin/Transform Margin new venture study. Emphesis on the evaluation of existing sub-commercial discoveries for potential development. Main diciplines include reservoir engineering and detailed volumetric calculations.
