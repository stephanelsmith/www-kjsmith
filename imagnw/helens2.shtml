Mt. St. Helens (while it was still with us)

The high winds seen clearly on this photo taken the morning after our ill-fated winter ascent of Mt. St. Helens. (See the story told in St Helens ). From the comfort of a lowland vantage the mountain looks tame and inviting, but the winds easily reach 75 to 100 miles per hour at the summit, let the climber beware.
