Smith greetings to all

Smith family while on a recent visit to the Texas Tech campus in Lubbock. Imagine finding a place in just about the middle of nowhere, then driving another 500 miles farther into nothingness. If you do that, you will find yourself in Lubbock. Although they say there are some 200,000 people living there, the town is physically smaller than Yakima and has a look and feel of a town that size. I suspect they are counting the cats, dogs, and chickens in the population. We were there during the cotton crop harvest and that was facinating to see. All the fields with the cotton puffs on the plants, cotton blowing along the roads, a veritable ocean of cotton.
